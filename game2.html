Decision Evaluation

The players will discuss the ethical challenges presented by a particular turn in the City development card (Preliminary Game or Pre-game). They need to analyze the consequences of these ethical challenges within the game and come up with a decision that aligns with an ethical code. They should also write down the impacts of their current decision and develop a plan for future development. Through various scenarios and tasks, the game aims to cultivate players' ethical awareness, moral decision-making abilities, and sense of responsibility.

Benefits

Through the game, players can encounter various moral and ethical dilemmas based on the specific circumstances analyzed in each turn, deepening their understanding and awareness of ethical issues. They can engage in decision-making and reflect upon different ethical principles and values.

Players are required to make their own decisions within the game, which helps exercise their moral judgment abilities. They need to weigh different ethical principles and considerations, making decisions that align with their own values and ethical standards. The choices and actions in the game lead to different consequences and impacts, allowing players to experience the outcomes of moral decisions. This helps them recognize the impact of their actions on others and the environment, fostering a sense of responsibility and moral accountability.

Players are encouraged to discuss and resolve ethical issues within their teams. This promotes teamwork, communication, and negotiation skills. Problem-solving within the game is both challenging and inspiring, stimulating players' thinking and discussions. They can reflect upon their own ethical beliefs, moral principles, and behavioral guidelines, as well as share and exchange ideas with others.

Preparation

Players are required to complete a pre-game in order to obtain some case studies for discussion. The preliminary game also helps players understand the background of the analyzed cases. Players will consider which ethical codes to apply and imagine the differences they would make if they were in control of the project.

Game Manual:

Game Objectives:

Learn ethical codes, understand the consequences of ethical challenges in decision-making. Enhance ethical awareness, develop moral judgment abilities, promote teamwork and discussion, cultivate a sense of responsibility and moral accountability.

    • Game Rules:

    Select a specific scenario through the preliminary game. Players discuss and analyze the ethical challenges within the scenario and engage in team discussions to provide improvement solutions.

    Role/Player Guide:

    Players can approach the game from the following perspectives:

    • 1.Respect
    • 2.Fairness
    • 3.Integrity
    • 4.Responsibility
    • 5.Efficiency and effectiveness
    • 6.Privacy
    • 7.Social responsibility

    During the Competition:

    During this competition, players will go through the following steps:

    • 1.Complete the preliminary game, and the host summarizes the overall game situation and generates discussion cases.
    • 2.Players draw a scenario.
    • 3.Evaluate from the perspective of ethical codes.
    • 4.Discuss opinions.
    • 5.Provide improvement solutions.

    Discussion

    The host asks each player to reflect on their shortcomings in the game, the decisions they made, and the ethical challenges involved in team cooperation.

    Next Steps:

    Reflect on situations in daily life and work where ethical codes were not followed. How to make more responsible and sustainable decisions in real life.
