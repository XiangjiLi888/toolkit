City Development and Decision Evaluation

  • City Development: is a problem-solving game that aims to help players develop and improve their systems thinking skills. Players take on the roles of different systems within a city and work towards the development of a realistic urban environment. The game presents various types of systems, including transportation departments, power utilities, city security departments, schools, hospitals, and more. Each system has unique characteristics and functions, and players need to understand how these systems interact and develop, while solving various challenges.The game presents a series of problems and challenges, and players need to apply the principles and methods of systems thinking to find the best solutions. They can optimize the structure of systems, improve resource allocation, and streamline processes to solve problems and enhance the efficiency and sustainability of the city's systems. Through collaboration and cooperation, players can collectively face complex system challenges, share experiences and insights, and work together to find the best solutions.
  • Decision Evaluation: The players will discuss the ethical challenges presented by a particular turn in the City development card (Preliminary Game or Pre-game). They need to analyze the consequences of these ethical challenges within the game and come up with a decision that aligns with an ethical code. They should also write down the impacts of their current decision and develop a plan for future development. Through various scenarios and tasks, the game aims to cultivate players' ethical awareness, moral decision-making abilities, and sense of responsibility.

Building an Effective Team

Building an Effective Team: is a management game that tests how players coordinate the cooperation of various employees, thereby helping players improve their ability to form an efficient company team. A company usually contains at least five categories of people, including managers, technical developers, salespeople, publicists, and production personnel. Players take on the role of the founder of a company, striving to improve the company's market performance and economic returns by recruiting, managing, and optimizing team members, balancing the company's expenses with product revenue each round, and responding to changes in the external environment. With limited resources, players need to develop strategies and decisions to achieve the goals set by the game in the set round.
